### 🧠 Who I Am

I'm a **full-stack developer** based in **Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia** building production software in the healthcare tech space. I work on [**AllMed**](https://songdo.allmed.mn) — a clinic management and appointment scheduling SaaS sold to hospitals and clinics across Mongolia — and I'm independently developing **Govi AI**, a multi-tenant AI chatbot platform for Mongolian businesses.

I write code that ships to real users, automates real workflows, and drives real revenue.
